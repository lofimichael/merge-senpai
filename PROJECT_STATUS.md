# Project Status

Merge Senpai's public hackathon edition is frozen as of July 10, 2026.

This repository remains public as a permanent record of the hackathon build.
Its review-first, repository-installed architecture is complete for that
purpose. Future commercial product development takes place separately.

## Maintenance policy

The repository does not accept new product features or architectural expansion.
The only changes that may be considered are:

- critical security fixes;
- changes required to keep the preserved demo from becoming actively harmful;
- corrections to materially inaccurate documentation.

Routine dependency upgrades, provider additions, feature requests, product
roadmap work, and hosted-service development are out of scope.

## Support

This repository is provided as a preserved hackathon artifact. It has no support
or compatibility commitment, and its GitHub App, third-party integrations, and
external model providers may stop working as those services evolve.

## Product separation

The private product is a separate codebase with a different contract: it turns
verified software changes into approved product stories and delivers them to
the appropriate audience. It does not extend this repository's code-review,
merge-gating, workflow-installation, or automated-fix paths.
